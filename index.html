    Digital Playbook Tutor ‚Äî User Guide

    1. Overview

    The Digital Playbook Tutor is an AI-supported learning tool designed to help linebackers study defensive calls, strengthen recognition skills, and practice decision-making through interactive study modes.

    2. How to Use Each Feature

    A. Playbook Explorer

    Use this tab to enter or upload your defensive plays. Each play includes:

    • Play name
    • Offensive formation (2x2 Spread)
    • Offensive concepts
    • Coverage
    • Blitz
    • Key reads
    • Mike/Will responsibilities

    These details populate all other study tools.

    B. Flashcards

    Flashcards convert your playbook entries into bite-sized concepts. Use them to:

    • Learn terminology
    • Memorize reads
    • Review assignments
    • Strengthen foundational knowledge

    Flip cards to reveal answers and move through the deck to build mastery.

    C. Quiz Mode

    The quiz checks understanding of each play in context:

    • Every question references a specific defensive call
    • Each item specifies the offensive formation: 2x2 Spread
    • Question types include multiple choice, true/false, and fill-in responses

    Use Quiz Mode regularly to reinforce memory and identify gaps.

    D. Scenario Drill

    This feature supports higher-order thinking:

    • New Scenario generates a situation tied directly to your stored plays
    • AI Scenario (LLM) generates a creative game-ready scenario using AI

    Scenarios may include motions, shifts, route combinations, or run threats that require linebackers to adjust and respond correctly.

    E. Prompts Log

    This section stores the AI prompts and responses generated during your study sessions. Use it for:

    • Reflection
    • Documentation
    • Understanding how AI supports learning

    F. Who This Tool Is For

    • Linebackers
    • Defensive players learning coverage structures
    • Coaches interested in AI-supported teaching
    • Students studying decision-making under pressure

    3. Ethical AI Use

    This tool is meant to supplement‚Äînot replace‚Äîcoaching instruction. Students should verify AI-generated outputs against coach-approved materials and remain critically aware of potential errors or oversimplifications.
